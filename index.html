Welcome to my website. I am a Pacific Northwestern web developer and software developer in Seattle, Washington. I love running, kayaking, going to breweries and walking my two dachshunds among many other activities. I am married to a wonderful man and we live together in harmony with our dachshunds Hansel and Wimbledon. I love African and Hawaiian coffees. Also, I love local beers from the northwest. I'm a complete locavore and I am inclined to select a local beer rather than a non-local beer. Thank you Pike Brewing Company in downtown Seattle, Odin's Brewing Company and Mac & Jack's Brewing Company in Redmond. Some of my favorite neighborhoods in Seattle are Capitol Hill, South Lake Union, Ballard, Belltown, University District, Magnolia, Fremont, Wallingford, and of course my home neighborhood. Also, I love spending time on the Kitsap Pensinula across the Puget Sound on Bainbridge Island and at my grandmother-in-law's home in Kingston, Washington.
